Request Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data from Naatey at any time.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and profile information
  • Your family tree and friendship connections
  • Your posts, recipes, and uploaded photos
  • Your matrimony profile (if created)
  • Your events, needs, and all activity data

What we retain (required by law)

  • Transaction records if any purchases were made
  • Data required by Indian law or court order
  • Anonymised analytics data (not linked to you)
1
Click the button below to send us a deletion request email from your registered email address
2
Include your registered phone number or full name in the email so we can identify your account
3
We will confirm your identity and process the deletion within 30 days as per our Privacy Policy
4
You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been permanently deleted
📧 Send Deletion Request

This will open your email app with a pre-filled deletion request.
Send it from the email address linked to your Naatey account.

⚠️ This action is permanent. Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Your family tree connections, recipes, and all content will be permanently removed.
