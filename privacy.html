Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 1 June 2026  ·  Last Updated: 1 June 2026
At Naatey, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Naatey app and related services. By using Naatey, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

Naatey ("we", "our", or "us") is a family and friends networking platform that connects people through bonds of family, friendship, matrimony, business, and shared heritage. Our registered contact email is hello@naatey.com and our website is naatey.com.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: Your name, phone number, date of birth, gender, and profile photo
  • Profile details: Location, profession, bio, heritage/cultural background, and relationship status
  • Family tree data: Names and relationships of family members and friends you add
  • Matrimony profile: Additional details you choose to share if you opt in to the matrimony section (education, preferences, etc.)
  • Content: Posts, comments, recipes, photos, event details, and needs you share on the platform
  • Communications: Messages and feedback you send to us

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage data: Features you use, screens you visit, and time spent on the app
  • Log data: IP address, access times, and error logs
  • Crash reports: Technical data when the app encounters errors

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google Sign-In: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile picture from Google
  • Phone verification: We use Firebase Authentication to verify your phone number via OTP

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Naatey account
  • To build and display your family and friendship tree
  • To show you relevant connections, matrimony matches, and business referrals within your network
  • To display your posts, recipes, and events to your chosen audience
  • To send push notifications about new connections, matches, and activity
  • To improve the app through usage analytics
  • To respond to your queries and provide customer support
  • To comply with legal obligations under Indian law including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With other Naatey users: Your profile, posts, and content are visible to people you connect with based on your privacy settings
  • With service providers: We use Google Firebase for authentication, database, and storage. These providers process data on our behalf under strict data protection agreements
  • For legal compliance: When required by Indian law, court order, or government authority
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred with appropriate notice to you

5. Your Privacy Controls

You have full control over your data on Naatey:

  • Visibility settings: Choose who sees your profile — family only, friends, or all connections
  • Matrimony opt-in: The matrimony section is entirely optional. You can enable or disable it at any time
  • Recipe visibility: Set each recipe to family-only, friends, or public
  • Edit or delete: Update or delete any content you post at any time
  • Account deletion: Delete your account and all associated data permanently from Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Data export: Request a copy of your data by emailing hello@naatey.com

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • All data is stored on Google Firebase servers with industry-standard encryption
  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS with TLS encryption
  • Phone number verification via OTP ensures only verified users access the platform
  • We regularly review our security practices to protect against unauthorised access

However, no system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at hello@naatey.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Naatey is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

9. Compliance with Indian Law

Naatey complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) of India. As a Data Fiduciary, we:

  • Collect only the data necessary for the purposes stated in this policy
  • Obtain your consent before processing sensitive personal data
  • Allow you to withdraw consent and request data deletion at any time
  • Maintain reasonable security safeguards to protect your data
  • Notify you in the event of a data breach that affects your personal data

10. Third-Party Links

The Naatey app may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any personal information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you via the app or by email. Continued use of Naatey after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
